Roasted root soup

Description

A blended soup made of roasted root vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Olive, grapeseed, or canola oil
  • 1 large white or yellow onion
  • 1 lb celery
  • 1 large rutabaga
  • 2-3 turnips
  • 1 lb parsnips
  • 1 lb carrots
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 3 bay leaves (optional)
  • 2 quarts stock or broth (optional)
  • Water

Tools

  • Oven
  • Stove
  • Cutting board and knife
  • Mixing bowl
  • Heat-safe silicon spatula or wooden spoon
  • 1-4 Half-sheet pans (same size as most home ovens)
  • Potholders, multiple, and someplace to cool your hot pans
  • Large soup pot
  • Immersion blender, food processor, or heat-safe blender

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400 F
  2. Dice vegetables as evenly as possible at 1/2 inch or 1 inch thickness

    3. Note: thinner pieces will roast faster

  3. Fill mixing bowl with diced root vegetables
  4. Pour in just enough oil to cover the veggies; mix well
  5. Spread onto pans in a single layer
  6. Roast in oven for 10 minutes
  7. Use spatula or wooden spoon to carefully flip veggies over
  8. Roast in oven for 10 more minutes
  9. Repeat steps as necessary

    10. Note: the goal is a nice golden brown on the edges and a soft middle

  10. While roasting root vegetables, cover the bottom of the soup pot with a few tablespoons of oil
  11. Set soup pot burner at medium heat, and add diced onion and celery once the oil is warmed
  12. Let the onions get glassy and the celery a little soft, then remove from heat
  13. Add roasted root vegetables to soup pot
  14. Add stock or broth to soup pot (optional)
  15. Fill soup pot with water up to an inch or two above root vegetables
  16. Add bay leaves, and bring soup to a simmer for at least 20 minutes before removing them (optional)
  17. Blend soup until smooth; add more water if necessary
  18. Add salt and pepper to taste

Variations

  • Try adding honey for some extra sweetness
  • Try adding some Cholula or Texas Pete if you like the spice

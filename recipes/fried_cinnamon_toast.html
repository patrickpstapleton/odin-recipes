Fried cinnamon toast

Description

No toaster? No problem! Fry that bread in a pan on the stove.

Ingredients

  • Sliced bread
  • Butter
  • Cinnamon sugar

Tools

  • Frying pan
  • Butter knife

Steps

  1. Set the pan on the stove, and set the burner just above medium
  2. Put about a teaspoon or so of butter in the pan to start melting
  3. Place a slice of bread directly onto the melting butter, and use your fingertips to gently drag the bread around the pan to evenly distrubute the butter as it melts
  4. Fry in pan 30-60 seconds, or until it's barely browned on the buttered side, then flip the bread over
  5. Sprinkle cinnamon sugar over the buttered side
  6. Fry in pan another 30-60 seconds, or until it has slightly browned on the non-buttered side
  7. Repeat until you have enough for breakfast

Optional

  • Try frying a sunny-side up egg in the same pan and using it as a topping for your cinnamon toast
